The way studying is meant to be

Scholarsome is your all-encompassing studying platform. Create study sets, import data from other platforms, take generated practice quizzes to test your knowledge, and much more. It's accessible from any device in a centralized, easy-to-use, web-based location.

Make your studying easy

With Scholarsome's tools at your disposal, you can go back to focusing on what matters: learning your sets efficiently.

Create randomized practice quizzes

Using the cards created in your sets, you can generate randomized quizzes to practice. Choose from a mixture of multiple choice, written, and true or false questions, depending on what the content of your set is best suited to.

Import and export to other platforms with ease

Sets can be easily imported into Scholarsome from a variety of other platforms - Quizlet, Anki, and importing from a CSV file. Plus, Scholarsome doesn't lock your data down. You're free to easily export your set data to those same platforms.

Use flashcards to enhance your memory

The cards created in your sets can be used in a familiar interface to study in the same way that you would with physical flashcards. Flip cards over, swap back and forth between them, and circle back to ones that you missed â€” all with convenient keybinds for long study sessions.

Feature-rich card editor

Each side of a card can be customized to your heart's content. Add multiple images, change the formatting of text, add LaTeX expressions, use code blocks, and much more.
